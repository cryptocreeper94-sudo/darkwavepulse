Fear & Greed Index

The Fear & Greed Index measures market emotions on a scale of 0-100. It combines volatility, market momentum, social media sentiment, surveys, dominance, and trends to show whether investors are too greedy or too fearful.

0-24: Extreme Fear - Market panic, potential buying opportunity

25-49: Fear - Investors worried, cautious sentiment

50-74: Greed - Optimistic market, watch for overvaluation

75-100: Extreme Greed - Euphoria, potential market top

"When everyone's greedy, I'm selling. When everyone's scared, I'm buying. It's really that simple, but you humans never learn." - Crypto Cat

Altcoin Season Index

The Altcoin Season Index shows whether it's "Bitcoin Season" or "Altcoin Season" by measuring how many of the top 50 altcoins outperformed Bitcoin in the last 90 days.

0-25: Bitcoin Season - BTC dominates, alts underperform

26-74: Mixed Season - Balanced performance

75-100: Altcoin Season - Alts are pumping hard!

"Altcoin season is when your portfolio goes 10x... right before it crashes 90%. Timing is everything, and yours is terrible." - Crypto Cat

Average RSI (Top 100)

The Relative Strength Index (RSI) measures momentum on a scale of 0-100. We average the RSI of the top 100 cryptocurrencies to gauge overall market momentum.

0-30: Oversold - Market potentially undervalued, bounce likely

30-70: Neutral - Healthy market conditions

70-100: Overbought - Market potentially overheated, correction coming

"RSI over 70? That's when amateurs FOMO in. RSI under 30? That's when they panic sell. I do the opposite, which is why I'm rich and you're... well, here." - Crypto Cat

Total Market Cap

Total cryptocurrency market capitalization represents the combined value of all cryptocurrencies. It's calculated by multiplying each coin's price by its circulating supply.

A rising market cap indicates overall market growth and increasing investor confidence. A declining market cap suggests bearish sentiment and potential downtrends.

Track the 24h change to see if money is flowing into or out of crypto as a whole.

"Market cap is up 5%? Cool. Wake me up when it's at 50% so I can start planning my exit. You'll still be HODLing at -80%." - Crypto Cat
