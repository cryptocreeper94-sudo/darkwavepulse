**What it does:** Schedule Twitter posts  
**Limit:** 10 scheduled posts at a time  
**Cost:** FREE

**Setup:**
1. Go to buffer.com

**Posting Schedule:**

**What it does:** Monitor mentions, schedule tweets

**Setup:**

   - #technicalanalysis hashtag

**Daily routine (15 min):**
